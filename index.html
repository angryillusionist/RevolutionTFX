Energy saving

Air-side economizers save energy by supplying cooler outside air to condition the indoor space. When the outside air is suitably cool and has a lower energy content than the indoor space, the dampers in the mixing box can be controlled in order to take advantage of the lower-energy outdoor air. Economizer configurations include barometric relief, powered exhaust and powered return fans.
